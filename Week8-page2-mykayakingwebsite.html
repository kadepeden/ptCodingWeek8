Welcome to the sport!

Kayaking is an amazing sport and an excellent way to get outside. If you are interested in getting into kayaking, you should look into buying the following safety gear:

  • White water rated kayakling helmet
  • A class V personal flotation device (PFD)
  • Throw bag, preferably spectra and 80ft in length
  • River knife
  • Personal water filtration system for inside of your PFD
  • Sunblock (recommended waterproof, 50 SPF or higher)
